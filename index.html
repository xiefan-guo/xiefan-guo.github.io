I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), supervised by Prof. Di Huang. My research interests focus on Computer Vision, Generative AI, and Diffusion models. Before that, I received my master degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), China, in Jan. 2023 and B.E degree from the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University (TJU), China, in Jul. 2020. I interned at Shanghai AI Laboratory, Alibaba TongYi Lab and Alibaba DAMO Academy.
